Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between AC and Non-AC rooms?

AC rooms are equipped with air conditioning for your comfort, while Non-AC rooms are budget-friendly with natural ventilation and a fan. Both have clean attached washrooms and daily housekeeping.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability. Contact us on WhatsApp or call to request!

Is WiFi really free for all guests?

Yes! We provide high-speed WiFi free of charge to all our guests. Perfect for staying connected during your stay.

How close is the railway station?

Sachin Railway Station is just a 5-minute walk away! We're in a perfect location for train travelers and industrial workers.

Can I book directly without using WhatsApp?

Absolutely! You can call us directly at 7383359660 to confirm your booking. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Are there any hidden charges?

No! What you see is what you pay. ₹900 for AC and ₹700 for Non-AC. No additional fees. Everything is transparent.
